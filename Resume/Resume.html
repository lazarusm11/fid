About Me

I am student a Seminole State College, studying for my assocites in arts. I want to study to become a concept designer, and character illustrator. I enjoy to use multiple mediums such as traditional sketching with graphite, colored pencils, water color paints, and alcholol markers. I have moved to primarily using digital media to create art with more freedom.

Digital media has allowed me to practice painting and free sketching in an entirely new way. I have taken virtual art courses to further improve my digital work. These courses covered topics and techniques like:

  • Landscape Painting
  • Iconography
  • Coloring and Painting complex designs
  • 3D modeling and rigging
  • Digital free-sketching
